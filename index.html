Few facts about the world we live in

Earth is the third planet from the Sun and the only astronomical object known to harbor life. The Earth is the only planet in our solar system known to support life. The Earth was formed about 4.5 billion years ago.

About the Earth

  • Earth is the densest planet in the Solar System.
  • Earth's atmosphere protects life on Earth by absorbing ultraviolet solar radiation and reducing temperature extremes between day and night.
  • Earth's gravity interacts with other objects in space, especially the Sun and the Moon, which is Earth's only natural satellite. This influence produces tides, stabilizes Earth's orientation on its axis, and gradually slows its rotation.
